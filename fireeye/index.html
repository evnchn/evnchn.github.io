How It Works

From Camera
to Actionable Alert

A three-stage pipeline — local detection, contextual reasoning, and smart alerting — all running on your existing camera infrastructure.

CAMERA INPUT
CCTV / Mobile
Existing Infra
Works with any IP camera, CCTV feed, or phone camera. No new hardware required. Supports telephoto, standard, wide-angle, and ultra-wide lenses with auto-detection.
49ms
12 CLASSES
YOLO DETECTION
YOLOv11n · Local GPU
Fire · Smoke · PPE · Gas
Fine-tuned on 6,100 construction site images. Detects fire, smoke, gas cylinders, welding sparks, fire extinguishers, scaffold nets, PPE helmets and vests, hose reels, exit signs, fire blankets, and tarpaulins. Runs entirely on-device.
route
Key Differentiator
🧠
VLM REASONING
Scene Context · Risk Level
Understands Why
A Vision Language Model reasons about the full scene: proximity of fuel sources, fire spread risk, regulatory compliance. Outputs a 5-level risk classification with natural-language explanation. The Future Agent predicts how the scene may evolve. Three-tier routing sends only ambiguous or high-stakes cases to the most capable model.
SMART ALERT
50ms Preliminary Alarm
Human-Readable
Instant 50ms alarm fires before VLM reasoning completes. Full report includes risk level, regulatory citations, spatial analysis, and actionable recommendations. Human-readable, not just a binary flag. Exportable for audit and compliance records.

Camera Health Diagnostics
Blocked Lens Dust Detection Camera Type <0.3ms
FireEye monitors the health of its own cameras in under 0.3ms per frame. Detects blocked lenses (8/8 scenarios, zero false positives), dust buildup via contrast baseline tracking, and auto-identifies camera type (telephoto / standard / wide / ultra-wide). The best fire detection means nothing if the camera is dusty.

Project History

How We Got Here

HKUST Final Year Project — two semesters of industry-grounded development.

Semester 1 — Oct 2025
Scaffolding Safety Training Platform
Built Scaffluent — an AI-powered training platform for Hong Kong's metal scaffolding transition. Developed an LLM-based assessment system using HK regulatory materials, and a computer vision module for scaffolding tube orientation verification. Engaged with 12+ industry stakeholders across construction and safety sectors.
Semester 2 — Feb 2026
Expanding to Live Site Monitoring
Expert feedback from scaffolding inspection practitioners revealed a broader opportunity: live monitoring of active construction sites. With the Wang Fuk Court fire (November 2025, 168 fatalities) exposing critical gaps in real-time fire detection on construction sites, the team focused on building FireEye — an AI monitoring system addressing this urgent need.
March 2026
FireEye — Live and Deployed
FireEye went from concept to live deployed system in five weeks. Custom-trained YOLO model (12 classes, HK construction-specific), three-stage VLM reasoning pipeline, camera health diagnostics, and 182 passing unit tests. Live at fireeye.evnchn.io with four active industry conversations.
Next — 2026
Industry Partnerships & Expansion
Active conversations with solution providers and construction tech partners. Exploring expansion into facilities management, PPE compliance, and broader construction safety verticals.
